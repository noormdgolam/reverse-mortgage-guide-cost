Understanding Proprietary Reverse Mortgage for South Dakota Seniors

This guide is designed to provide detailed information on proprietary reverse mortgage options available to eligible seniors in South Dakota. Reverse mortgages are specialized loan programs that allow homeowners to tap into their home's equity without selling or relocating from their property.

What is a Proprietary Reverse Mortgage?

A proprietary reverse mortgage is a non-HUD (United States Department of Housing and Urban Development) insured loan that is offered by private lenders. These loans are typically designed to address specific requirements or circumstances of borrowers and may offer more flexibility in loan terms and repayment options than traditional HUD-insured reverse mortgages.

Eligibility Requirements for Proprietary Reverse Mortgage in South Dakota

To be eligible for a proprietary reverse mortgage, borrowers typically must be at least 62 years old, have owned their home for at least six months, and occupy the property as their principal residence. South Dakota seniors will need to provide financial information and documentation about their loan options to compare these types of loans with traditional options.

Benefits of Proprietary Reverse Mortgage for South Dakota Seniors

  • Larger loan amounts - Proprietary reverse mortgages may offer more flexible qualification rules and potentially larger loan amounts, enabling borrowers to tap into their home's equity and meet financial goals.
  • Flexible repayment options - Unlike traditional reverse mortgages that require monthly payments, proprietary reverse mortgages may allow repayment on a loan without monthly mortgage insurance premiums, or may offer cash flow benefits through lump sums, annuities, or lines of credit.
  • No required monthly mortgage payments - Borrowers who take out proprietary reverse mortgages usually do not have to make regular mortgage payments, providing financial relief during retirement or old age.

Types of Proprietary Reverse Mortgage in South Dakota

  • HeirLine - HeirLine is a proprietary reverse mortgage that provides for fixed monthly payments to senior borrowers. It is provided by a company that is not HUD-related but is a licensed bank.
  • HomeSafe** - HomeSafe** is a proprietary reverse mortgage that is available to eligible borrowers. The loan offers a lump sum that may be used by the borrower.

Carefully Evaluate and Compare Loan Offers

South Dakota seniors must carefully evaluate loan options and consider seeking professional advice to ensure that a proprietary reverse mortgage meets their individual needs and financial goals. It's highly recommended to compare HUD loans and proprietary loans before making any final decisions.

Prior to selecting and applying for proprietary reverse mortgage, South Dakota seniors are encouraged to review their home equity and compare multiple loan offers to ensure the best financial options for their situation are considered.

Contact a HUD-Approved Counselor in South Dakota

Before choosing a proprietary reverse mortgage, seniors in South Dakota are recommended to meet a HUD-approved counselor to discuss all of their financing options. It is also recommended that South Dakota seniors consult the Federal Trade Commission website to understand how to avoid scams in reverse mortgage loans.
