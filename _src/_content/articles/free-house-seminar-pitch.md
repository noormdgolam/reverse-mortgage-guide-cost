---\n\nIf you are over the age of 62, you have likely received a glossy invitation in the mail inviting you to a free steak dinner at a local restaurant to learn about a "Government Benefit Program for Seniors." 

These seminars are often high-pressure sales pitches designed to sell you a reverse mortgage using highly deceptive language.

## The Deceptive Pitch

The salesmen running these seminars are masters of psychology. They rarely use the term "Reverse Mortgage" because they know it has a negative stigma. Instead, they use terms like:
- "HECM Government Benefit"
- "Home Equity Retirement Grant"
- "The Free House Strategy"

They will stand at the front of the room and promise that the federal government wants to give you tax-free money, and that you will never have to pay it back. They make it sound like a lottery winning that you are entitled to because of your age.

## The Reality They Hide

A reverse mortgage is **not** a government benefit. It is a loan provided by a private bank. The government (via the FHA) merely insures the loan; they are not giving you free money. 

The most dangerous lie is the concept of the "free house." The salesman will tell you that you never have to make a payment, implying the house is now free. 

What they fail to emphasize is the **Homeowner Obligations**. You are still strictly required to pay your county property taxes and your homeowners insurance. If you leave the seminar believing the house is entirely "free" and stop paying your property taxes, you will quickly find yourself facing foreclosure and eviction.

## How to Handle the Seminar

There is nothing wrong with attending the seminar and eating the free steak dinner. But you must approach the information with extreme skepticism.

- **Do not sign any paperwork** at the seminar, and do not allow the salesman to schedule an "in-home consultation" immediately.
- If you are genuinely interested in the product, take their informational packet and review it with a trusted family member or financial advisor. 
- Most importantly, remember that you are legally required to attend independent **HUD Counseling** before you can get a reverse mortgage. The independent counselor will strip away the salesman's hype and explain the mathematical reality of the loan.\n
