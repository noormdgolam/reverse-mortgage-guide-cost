## Understanding Reverse Mortgage Costs

One of the most critical aspects of securing a reverse mortgage is understanding the upfront and ongoing costs. Unlike traditional mortgages, reverse mortgages have unique fee structures mandated by the Federal Housing Administration (FHA).

We have compiled dozens of in-depth guides to help you navigate these expenses:

- **Origination Fees:** Learn how lenders calculate this fee and strategies to negotiate it down.
- **Upfront Mortgage Insurance Premium (MIP):** Understand why you pay a 2% upfront premium and how it guarantees you will never owe more than your home is worth.
- **Third-Party Closing Costs:** A breakdown of title insurance, recording fees, and counseling costs.
- **The Appraisal:** Why FHA appraisals cost more and what to expect during the inspection process.
