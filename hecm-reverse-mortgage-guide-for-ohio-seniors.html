Understanding HECM Reverse Mortgage in Ohio

The Home Equity Conversion Mortgage (HECM) is a unique type of loan designed specifically for homeowners aged 62 and older in Ohio. This government-insured reverse mortgage program allows homeowners to borrow funds using the equity in their primary residence. In this guide, we will explore the intricacies of the HECM reverse mortgage program, addressing key considerations, eligibility, benefits, risks, and costs associated with this financial solution.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Age**: Homeowners must be at least 62 years old to apply for the HECM reverse mortgage in Ohio.
  • Property Ownership**: The property must be the borrower's primary residence, which means it cannot be rented out or serve as a second home.
